In our commitment to providing a safe and secure environment for both riders and drivers, our Uber-like taxi application strictly adheres to a comprehensive set of safety rules and guidelines. All drivers undergo thorough background checks, ensuring a trustworthy and reliable experience for our users. To further prioritize safety, we enforce a zero-tolerance policy for any form of discrimination, harassment, or inappropriate behavior. Additionally, our platform incorporates real-time GPS tracking, allowing loved ones to monitor the journey, and all transactions are cashless to eliminate potential risks. Both riders and drivers are expected to adhere to traffic laws, and any violation may result in immediate suspension from the platform. With the collective responsibility of our community, we strive to create a secure and enjoyable transportation experience for everyone who chooses our service.
